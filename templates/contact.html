     My name is Melinda Michael. I am an actress and voice over artist. I started in theatre, performing on stage in plays, musicals, and dance recitals. I currently specialize in the film, television, and commercial industry. Let me share with you some of my unique qualities that set me apart from the rest.

 

My background: I have quite a complex mix of ethnicities, making my look very interesting, exotic, and versatile. I am proud to be Italian, Lebanese, Antiguan, British, Welsh, and Irish.

 

My interests: volunteering at a meditation studio where I lead the musical group meditations, kickboxing, dance (lyrical, ballet, hip hop, jazz, salsa), singing, skipping (I was on the Heart and Stroke Foundation skipping team that travelled to elementary schools teaching kids how to skip to encourage a healthy, active lifestyle), hot yoga, languages (I have studied Italian, Spanish and French), traveling, modeling (every photographer I have worked with comments on my excellent body/muscle awareness, and how I make postural adjustments instinctively), and most profoundly, ACTING! (I have trained since the age of 5 in theatre, and am now focusing fully on working as an on-camera actor.) 

 

I am truly excited and grateful to be expanding myself as an actor and as a unique individual within a creative and wonderfully talented community. It gives me so much joy to work every day towards manifesting my biggest dreams and career aspirations.  I have what it takes: the ambition, passion, presence, talent, motivation, determination, and amiable personality to succeed in this industry. I strive to give my all, always.
